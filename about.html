About Rhema Health Care

Rhema Health Care is a UK-based healthcare services organisation delivering compassionate, person-centred care. We partner with families, GPs and hospitals to provide tailored support.

Our mission

To improve health outcomes by delivering consistent, respectful and clinically excellent care where and when people need it.

  • • Dignity and respect in all interactions
  • • Evidence-based clinical practice
  • • Strong links with local NHS and community services

Our values

We are guided by compassion, competence and accountability.

  1. Compassion: putting people first
  2. Quality: clinical excellence and continual improvement
  3. Collaboration: working with partners for better care

Our team

Our multidisciplinary teams include registered nurses, carers, physiotherapists and administrative staff — all DBS-checked and trained in up-to-date clinical standards.
